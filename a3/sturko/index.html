Sturkö

Background

In 1981, a Soviet submarine was discovered in the morning just outside the Swedish naval base, it claimed it was a navigation error but things quickly escalated. The Russians sent a "rescue" party while the Swedish primeminister gave the order "Hold the border", instructing the military to shoot if any ships crossed the border.

Luckily, after a few days the submarine was returned to Russia and the crises was over, but this was the Swedish "cuban crises".

More information here: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Soviet_submarine_S-363
