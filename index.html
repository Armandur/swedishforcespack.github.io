Swedish Forces Pack

Description

Includes features such as advance missiles.buddy reload, backblast, "random" numbers on helicopters. More than 11 new completely new vehicles, including airplane, helicopter, drones, jeeps and boats. Over 20 new weapons and to enjoy all this are over 15 missions for 2-40 players. Coop and PvP.

Divided into three era's, 1990's, 2000-2015 and a more futuristic 2035. In the modern day scenario you find both woodland and desert soldiers.
